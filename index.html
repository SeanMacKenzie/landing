bio

Sean MacKenzie grew up in Boise, Idaho and attended Boise State University. He graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in English with a Writing Emphasis and has been working for the St. Luke's Health System for the past 15 years as a Dispatcher for the Internal Transporation Department.

His fascination with computers and his love of puzzles led him to believe that computer programming would be a better occupational trajectory for himself and his family. In the fall of 2017, he decided to attend Boise CodeWorks and make a complete career change. At Boise CodeWorks, he learned the skills necessary to become a full-stack developer.

Sean is a husband and father of 3 amazing children and when he is not working, he enjoys reading, writing, and playing games.
